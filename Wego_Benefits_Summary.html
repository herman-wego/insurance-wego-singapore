How to Claim

Outpatient & Specialist Claims

Panel visits: Use your medical card (cashless) — no claims needed.

Non-panel visits (reimbursement):
1. Get a GP referral for specialist claims
2. Collect original itemised bills/receipts
3. Include referral letter for specialist/X-ray/lab claims
4. Include prescription for drug purchases
5. Submit within 30 days of discharge

Hospital & Surgical Claims

Planned admission: Request eLOG via GREAT Med+ app for cashless pre-approval.

Claims documents needed:
1. Original final & detailed hospital bill
2. Pre & post hospitalisation invoices
3. Ambulatory / pre-admission form
4. Inpatient discharge summary
5. GP referral letter & lab referral forms
6. Submit within 30 days of discharge
📱 All reimbursement claims can be submitted through the E-Benefits Portal.
